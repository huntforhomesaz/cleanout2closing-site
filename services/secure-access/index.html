Secure Access Management

Keys, lockbox codes, alarm systems. We manage property access so you don't have to coordinate every vendor visit.

Your Peace of Mind

One Point of Contact for Every Access Need

When multiple vendors need access to a property — cleanout crews, contractors, inspectors, appraisers, photographers, and buyers' agents — coordinating who gets keys, when, and how quickly becomes a full-time job. Especially if you're managing it from another state.

Cleanout2Closing handles all property access coordination. We serve as your local representative, ensuring the right people get in at the right times while maintaining the security and integrity of the property.

Key & Lockbox Management

We handle physical key distribution and maintain secure lockbox access codes, updating them as vendor needs change throughout the process.

Alarm System Coordination

We work with existing alarm systems or arrange temporary security solutions to protect the property throughout the transition.

Vendor Scheduling

Rather than you fielding calls from every contractor, we coordinate scheduling and ensure every vendor access is accounted for.

Out of state families: This service is especially valuable when you cannot be physically present. We become your trusted local presence, accountable for every person who accesses the property.
