Estate Cleanout Coordination

We coordinate the sorting, removal, and donation of personal belongings. You make the decisions; we handle the logistics and labor coordination.

What This Means

Your Family Makes the Decisions. We Handle the Rest.

Clearing a lifetime of belongings from an estate property is one of the most emotionally and logistically challenging parts of any property transition. There's no simple way to do it — but there is a more organized, less stressful way.

Cleanout2Closing handles the coordination so you don't have to manage multiple vendors, schedules, and logistics from a distance. We bring order to the process while being sensitive to the nature of the work.

What We Coordinate

Sorting & Inventory

We help organize belongings into keep, donate, sell, and remove categories based on your decisions as the family.

Donation Coordination

We coordinate pickups with local Tucson charities and organizations, ensuring usable items go to good causes.

Estate Sale Referrals

When an estate sale is appropriate, we can refer you to qualified professionals and help coordinate access.

Junk Removal

Items that cannot be donated or sold are removed efficiently. We coordinate reputable local removal services.

Out of state? We serve as your local presence. You make decisions; we execute them — coordinating every step with regular photo updates and clear communication.
