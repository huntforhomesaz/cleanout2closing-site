Documentation & Reporting

Detailed photo documentation, progress reports, and expense tracking. Everything you need to stay informed and make decisions — from wherever you are.

Transparency First

You're Always Informed. Always in Control.

Trust sales and probate transactions often require clear documentation trails. Family members making decisions from a distance need reliable information to make sound choices. And trustees have fiduciary obligations that require proper record-keeping.

We build documentation into every step of the process — not as an afterthought, but as a core part of how we work. You always know what's happening at the property, what decisions are pending, and what work has been completed.

Photo Documentation

Before and after photos of every phase of work — cleanout, repairs, cleaning, and final preparation. A visual record of the property's transformation.

Progress Reports

Regular written updates on what's been completed, what's in progress, and what's coming next. You're never left wondering what's happening.

Expense Tracking

Clear records of vendor costs, materials, and expenses throughout the process. Especially important for trust administration and estate accounting.

For trustees and attorneys: Our documentation supports proper trust administration and probate reporting. We coordinate on the property side while your legal team handles fiduciary matters.
