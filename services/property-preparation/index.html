Property Preparation

From minor repairs to deep cleaning, we coordinate the vendors needed to get the property market-ready. No job too big or too small.

Getting It Right

Market-Ready Without the Headache

Estate and inherited properties often need work before they're ready to sell. Deferred maintenance, dated fixtures, deep cleaning needs — these are common challenges that can feel overwhelming, especially when you're managing it from a distance.

Bradley's background with Grunt-Work LLC means he can assess what truly needs to be done versus what's optional, helping you prioritize work that maximizes value without overspending. He's personally overseen hundreds of projects across Tucson and knows which vendors do quality work at fair prices.

What We Coordinate

Repairs & Maintenance

From leaky faucets to HVAC service, we identify and coordinate necessary repairs to meet buyer expectations and inspection standards.

Deep Cleaning

Professional cleaning inside and out. Kitchens, bathrooms, carpets, windows — we coordinate thorough cleaning crews with estate experience.

Painting & Touch-Ups

Interior and exterior painting where needed. Fresh paint is one of the highest-ROI improvements for any listing.

Landscaping

Curb appeal matters. We coordinate yard cleanup, desert landscaping maintenance, and exterior improvements that make first impressions count.

Staging Recommendations

We advise on furniture placement, decluttering, and simple staging to help buyers envision themselves in the space.

Vendor Oversight

We don't just hand you a list of contractors. Bradley personally oversees the work, ensures quality, and keeps the project on schedule.

Why this matters: When something unexpected comes up — and it usually does — Bradley knows how to handle it because he's solved those problems before.
