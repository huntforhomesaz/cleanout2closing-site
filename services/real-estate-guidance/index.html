Real Estate Guidance

As a licensed Realtor backed by Tucson's top team, Bradley provides market analysis, pricing guidance, and manages the entire sale process from listing to close.

The Full Picture

Not Just a Sale — A Complete Transition

When you hire a traditional real estate agent for an estate sale, you're hiring someone to manage the transaction. The weeks of work that come before — the cleanout, the repairs, the vendor coordination — are still your responsibility.

Bradley is different because he manages both. By the time a property is listed, he already knows every corner of the home, every repair that was made, and exactly how to position it in the market. That integrated knowledge creates a stronger listing.

Market Analysis

A thorough comparative market analysis specific to estate and inherited property sales in Tucson — accounting for condition, location, and current market dynamics.

Strategic Pricing

Pricing that reflects the property's true market value after preparation, maximizing the net proceeds without leaving money on the table.

Professional Marketing

Professional photography, MLS listing, digital marketing, and exposure through the Stratton Group's established buyer network across Tucson.

Showings & Negotiation

We manage all showing coordination and handle buyer negotiations with experience, transparency, and your best interests as the priority.

Escrow & Closing

From accepted offer through closing, we guide you through every step of the escrow process, coordinating with title, escrow, and all parties.

Stratton Group Partnership

Backed by Tucson's top real estate team — over 400 families helped in 2024 alone, with listings going under contract faster than the market average.

Important: This is the final step of the Cleanout2Closing process, not a standalone service. When Bradley lists your property, it's because he's guided it through every prior stage. That's the advantage.
