## Common questions from out-of-state owners and heirs

### How many trips to Tucson will I need to make?

In many cases, none.

If you want to attend certain milestones in person (initial walkthrough, estate sale, or closing), we can plan around that.  
But the process can usually be designed so that you do not have to travel unless you choose to.

### What if there are tenants or other occupants in the home?

Occupancy brings legal considerations.

Your attorney, property manager, or local legal counsel will guide decisions about notices, access, and timing.  
Within that guidance, I can:

- Coordinate inspections and vendor visits.  
- Plan the eventual transition of the property toward sale.  
- Help you understand how different occupancy scenarios affect pricing and timing.

### What if I am not ready to sell yet?

You can still reach out.

We can:

- Document the current condition.  
- Address urgent issues such as leaks, break-ins, or code concerns.  
- Outline steps to stabilize the property until you are ready.  
- Rough out what a future path to sale would look like.

You do not have to commit to a timeline before we talk.
