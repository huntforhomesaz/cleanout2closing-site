# Green Valley, AZ service area

Green Valley is one of the most retiree-heavy communities in Arizona.  
Roughly 80% of residents are 65 or older, with a median age around 73 and very few households with children.   

From a real estate perspective, that means a high share of home sales are tied to:

- Assisted living and higher-level care moves  
- Downsizing and senior transitions  
- Estates, trusts, and inherited property  
- Out-of-state owners and snowbirds deciding to sell

## Why Green Valley is a priority service area

Green Valley has been developed and marketed as an active-adult and retirement community since its inception, with many age-restricted neighborhoods where at least one occupant must be 55+ and no permanent residents under 18.   

Key realities that matter for your situation:

- Very high 65+ share (around 79.8% of residents) and median age over 73.   
- Large number of 55+ and age-restricted communities, HOAs, and active adult developments.   
- Strong tie-in to Tucson for hospitals, specialists, and many professional services.  
- Many homes owned by retirees, snowbirds, or out-of-state families.

That mix makes Green Valley a perfect match for an **estate transition concierge** model rather than a traditional, one-size-fits-all real estate approach.

## How Cleanout2Closing works in Green Valley

The steps look similar across most Green Valley cases:

1. **Clarify roles and goals**  
   - Who is legally in charge (owner, PR, trustee, fiduciary)  
   - Whether an attorney or care team is already involved  
   - Basic goals: free up funds, reduce work, protect family relationships

2. **Document the property**  
   - On-site walkthrough and photos  
   - Quick check for leaks, roof issues, HVAC, and HOA concerns  
   - Simple notes that can be shared with attorneys, fiduciaries, or advisors

3. **Plan contents and vendors**  
   - Contents plan: keep, ship, donate, sell, discard  
   - Vendor mix: cleanout, donation pickup, estate sale, yard work, cleaners, basic trades  
   - Clear approvals so everyone knows what has been authorized

4. **Prepare, list, and sell**  
   - Right-sized prep (usually clean, safe, and honest rather than a full remodel)  
   - Pricing and listing strategy tuned to Green Valley's 55+ buyer base  
   - Offer review, negotiation, and escrow coordination

Throughout, you get one accountable point of contact on the property side, and your attorney or fiduciary stays in charge of the legal and accounting file.
