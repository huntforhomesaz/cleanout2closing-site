Marana Service Area

Serving All of Marana

Marana has grown significantly over the past two decades, developing from a quiet agricultural community into one of the most sought-after areas in the greater Tucson region. Communities like Dove Mountain, Continental Ranch, and Saguaro Ranch feature homes of varying ages and conditions — from newer builds with HOA requirements to established properties that may need more substantial preparation before sale.

Bradley Hunt serves all of Marana, bringing the same integrated estate transition process that families across Pima County rely on. He manages vendor coordination, property access, cleanout logistics, and the full real estate sale — so Marana families don't have to juggle everything themselves.
