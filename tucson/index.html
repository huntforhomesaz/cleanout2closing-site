We Know Tucson

Tucson is a city of distinct neighborhoods — from the historic Sam Hughes and Barrio Historico to the sprawling eastside communities near Vail, the foothills near Catalina, and the midtown corridors. Estate properties come in all shapes, sizes, and conditions across all of these areas.

Bradley Hunt has managed estate cleanouts and property transitions throughout Tucson for years. He understands which neighborhoods attract which buyers, what condition matters most to the Tucson market, and which local vendors do quality work at fair prices.

Whether the property is a 1950s ranch home near the university, a sprawling foothills estate, or a midcentury modern near downtown, Cleanout2Closing has the local knowledge to manage the transition effectively.
