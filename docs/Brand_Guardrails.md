## 1. Brand Role & Positioning (LOCKED)

**Brand Name:** Cleanout2Closing  
**Abbreviation:** C2C (internal shorthand only)

**Brand Role**  
Cleanout2Closing is the **consumer-facing estate transition brand** under the Bradley Hunt Estate Transitions umbrella.

**Core Promise**  
One accountable professional coordinating everything from cleanout to closing.

**What C2C Is**
- Realtor-led estate transition service  
- Vendor coordination + real estate execution  
- Single point of accountability through escrow  

**What C2C Is NOT**
- A junk removal company  
- A moving company  
- An estate sale company  
- A labor crew  
- A marketplace or referral list  

C2C coordinates and oversees vendors. It does not perform labor services directly.
