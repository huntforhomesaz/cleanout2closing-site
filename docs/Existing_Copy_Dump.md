How It Works (Process Narrative)
1. Initial Consultation

We start with a conversation to understand the situation, timeline, and goals. This helps determine the right next steps.

2. Property Walkthrough

An on-site or virtual walkthrough allows us to assess the property, contents, and any immediate concerns.

3. Cleanout Coordination

We coordinate cleanout services, sorting needs, donations, disposal, and secure handling of the property.

4. Property Preparation

Repairs, cleaning, landscaping, and staging coordination are managed based on the condition and goals for sale.

5. Marketing and Sale

As a licensed Realtor, we guide pricing, marketing, negotiations, and escrow through closing.

FAQ Language (Approved)
How is this different from a traditional Realtor?

Most Realtors focus only on the sale. Cleanout2Closing coordinates cleanout, vendors, and property preparation in addition to real estate guidance.

Do you handle everything yourself?

No. We coordinate and oversee qualified vendors while serving as the single point of contact for the client.

Can you help if I live out of state?

Yes. Many clients are out of state. We manage local coordination and provide updates so you are not flying back and forth.

How long does the process take?

Every situation is different. Timelines depend on the property condition, legal requirements, and family decisions.

Do you guarantee a sale price or timeline?

No guarantees are made. We provide guidance based on market conditions and property factors.

Is this only for probate situations?

No. We assist with probate, trust sales, inherited homes, senior transitions, and family property situations.
